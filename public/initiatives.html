Our Events

Women and Valour

The college community converged in a poignant moment of reverence during the "Women and Valour" event. This dual tribute paid homage to the indomitable spirit of Rani Laxmi Bai on Balidan Diwas while also honouring the unwavering dedication of our present-day female Security and Central Housekeeping Staff (CHS) members. Taking place at the dignified Senate Hall on June 18, 2023, from 11:00 AM to 12:00 PM, the event was graced by the esteemed presence of DSP Annu Kumari, who adorned the occasion as the guest of honour. Her aura mirrored the resolute ethos of courage and commitment.

Asian Parliamentary Debate

The Indian Institute of Technology, Patna orchestrated a captivating oratory event, drawing enthusiastic student participation. Titled "India: A Tapestry of Philosophical Traditions," the Asian Parliamentary Debate (APD) aimed to dissect Indian culture and society from diverse angles, fostering critical thinking and layered perspectives. The competition prompted participants to transcend conventional boundaries and explore India's spiritual consciousness juxtaposed with the West's materialistic influence.

International Yoga Day

In a harmonious collaboration with the Wellness Center and Students' Welfare Board, our institute embraced the essence of well-being on June 21, 2023, to commemorate International Yoga Day. The day commenced at 5 A.M. yoga session, drawing over 350 enthusiastic participants who wholeheartedly embraced the teachings of yoga. Prof. T N Singh, our Honourable Director, eloquently highlighted yoga's holistic significance, casting a resonant tone for the day's proceedings....

Nukkad Natak

On August 5, 2023, Patna Junction witnessed a captivating street play organised by Indian Institute of Technology Patna students. This insightful performance was a part of the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative. The play illuminated prevailing societal issues—corruption, sexual violence, discrimination (gender, caste, religion), and dowry. Each act presented the consequences of these problems, provoking introspection among the audience. It earned accolades from figures like Station Director Shri Raju Kumar, recognizing its power to address crucial issues and ignite change.

Shastrarth - An Open India Quiz

"Shastrarth" Open India Quiz at IIT Patna, inspired by the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative, celebrates India's vibrant heritage and intellectual tradition. This unique event invites participants to explore the country's diverse legacy, spanning topics such as history, culture, science, and more. By reviving the ancient tradition of scholarly debate, "Shastrarth" promotes unity in diversity, fostering a spirit of intellectual curiosity and thought-provoking discussion. The event serves as a bridge between tradition and innovation, embodying the essence of India's rich cultural tapestry.

Water Painting Competition

IIT Patna's "Vibrant Visions of India" event, part of the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative, took place on August 9, 2023, in the Exhibition Room. This water painting competition brought together participants to artistically represent their home states' unique traditions and ethos. Using brushes and paints, they captured India's rich cultural tapestry. Esteemed judges evaluated the artworks on creativity and thematic relevance, awarding those who excelled. The event celebrated India's unity in diversity, promoting cultural appreciation and fostering creative expression through art.

Cyclothon event

IIT Patna's Cyclothon event, held on August 15 at 6:30 AM, was a vibrant part of the "Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat" campaign. Spanning 5-6 km, the event promoted healthier living, sustainable transportation, and cultural integration. Participants, including students, faculty, staff, and locals, cycled through a route adorned with cultural stalls, banners, and performances, celebrating India's diverse heritage. Safety and traffic rules were strictly followed, ensuring a seamless experience. The event's success highlighted the unifying power of such initiatives, fostering community and a commitment to well-being.

Volunteer Day Cleanliness Drive

IIT Patna's cleanliness drive on August 14, 2023, under the Ek Bharat Shreshtha Bharat initiative, showcased unity and community engagement. From 6:00 PM to 8:00 PM, volunteers cleaned the campus and adjacent areas, restoring green spaces, conducting awareness campaigns, and painting walls. The drive's success brought cleaner surroundings and heightened environmental awareness, emphasizing the importance of collective effort. This event not only improved the campus aesthetics but also fostered a sense of responsibility, inspiring ongoing commitment to a cleaner, united community...

Essay and Poem Writing Competition

The "Independence Day Essay and Poem Writing Competition," orchestrated by the Indian Institute of Technology (IIT) Patna, stood as a celebration of India's Independence Day intertwined with the promotion of creative expression. The event encompassed two categories: essay and poem writing, inviting participation both on campus and online, with outdoor and indoor recitations and assessments....

LensFlare: Captured Visions

IIT Patna orchestrated a captivating photography exhibition titled "LensFlare: Captured Visions." Aligned with the essence of Independence Day, the event aimed to celebrate India's diversity through students' artistic talent and unique perspectives in photography. The exhibition, held at the Gymkhana on August 15, harmoniously blended creativity and cultural awareness. Students submitted photographs reflecting various themes resonating with India's unity in diversity, spanning landscapes, architecture, festivals, and traditions.
